Meet Henry

Way back in 2015, Chris and Gerry, we'll call them 'Kerry', first spotted a young raptor whilst gallivanting around the park, as two young men do. After Snapchatting, Facebooking, and Instagramming the jurassic sighting, the duo cautiously approached the orange Velociraptor.

"Henry's my name and Charity is my game", screeched the reptile (apparently rhyming is his game as well). Completely shocked by the walking, talking orange Velociraptor, they asked Henry, "How can we help?"

Having been banned from nearly every charitable organization, Henry's dream of giving to charity was becoming extinct faster than the dinosaurs…too soon? Imagine trying to build a house without opposable thumbs, or the disapproving looks he received at the Food Bank where he ate more than he served, and don't even get Henry started with the Humane Society fiasco…

In an effort to help Henry, Chris & Gerry (Kerry) came up with a way to give Men, Women & Velociraptors alike, the opportunity to raise money for deserving causes & charities with a simple swipe of a raptor claw! Thus, VelociRaffle was born!
